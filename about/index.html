About me#

Hey you, all! So you want to know a little about me, well this is a short story because my work represents me.#

  • Bachelor in Physics at the Universidad Autónoma del Estado de Morelos
  • Master in Physical Sciences at the Universidad Nacional Nacional Autónoma de México
  • PhD in Optics at the Instituto Nacional de Astrofísica, Óptica y Electrónica
